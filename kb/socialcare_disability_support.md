title: Disability support and adaptations (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [social-care, disability, adaptations, support]
summary:
- Support available for disabled residents including adaptations and equipment.
- Apply for assessment to determine eligibility.

key_points:
- Request an occupational therapy assessment for home adaptations.
- Adaptations may include: ramps, handrails, stairlifts, bathroom modifications.
- Minor adaptations provided free; major works may require financial assessment.
- Disabled Facilities Grant available for major adaptations.
- Equipment like wheelchairs and walking aids available through NHS.
- Blue Badge scheme for parking concessions.
- Accessible housing register for those needing adapted homes.
- Support groups and advice services available.
