tags: [social-care, mental-health, support, wellbeing]
summary:
- Mental health support available for adults and children.
- Crisis support available for urgent situations.

key_points:
- Contact your GP for mental health concerns and referrals.
- Sussex Partnership NHS Trust provides mental health services.
- Crisis team available 24/7 for urgent mental health needs.
- Wellbeing services offer counselling and therapy.
- Support groups and peer support available across the city.
- Mental health helpline available for advice and information.
- Council social workers can provide support for those with severe mental illness.
- Emergency: call 999 or go to A&E for life-threatening situations.
