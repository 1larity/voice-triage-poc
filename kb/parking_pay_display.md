title: Pay and display parking (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [parking, pay-and-display, short-stay]
summary:
- Pay and display bays are available for short-term parking.
- Payment can be made by phone, app, or at machines.

key_points:
- Parking charges vary by location and duration.
- Maximum stay limits apply in many bays (typically 1-4 hours).
- Pay using the RingGo app, by phone, or at pay and display machines.
- Display ticket clearly on dashboard if using machines.
- Check signs for specific restrictions and operating hours.
- Some bays are free after 6pm or on Sundays.
- Blue badge holders may have free or reduced parking.
- Don't overstay - fines apply for exceeding time limits.
