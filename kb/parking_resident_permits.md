title: Resident parking permits (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [parking, resident-permit, parking-zone]
summary:
- Residents can apply for parking permits to park in their zone.
- Permits are charged annually and require proof of residency.

key_points:
- Apply online through MyAccount or by post.
- Cost varies by vehicle type and zone; typically around £40-£100 per year.
- Need proof of address (council tax bill, utility bill) and vehicle registration.
- One permit per vehicle; maximum of 2 permits per household.
- Permits are valid for 12 months.
- Visitor permits are separate from resident permits.
- Electric vehicles may be eligible for discounted permits.
- Check your parking zone on the council website.
