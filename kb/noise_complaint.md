title: Report a noise complaint (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [noise, noise-complaint, nuisance, report]
summary:
- Report noise problems to the council for investigation.
- Keep records of incidents to support your complaint.

key_points:
- Report noise online or by phone to the environmental health team.
- Provide details: type of noise, when it happens, and how it affects you.
- Keep a diary of incidents with dates and times.
- Council may install monitoring equipment to gather evidence.
- Common issues: loud music, DIY, barking dogs, alarms.
- Mediation may be offered to resolve disputes.
- Legal action can be taken if noise continues.
- Emergency noise: call police on 999 if immediate danger.
