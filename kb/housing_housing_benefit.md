title: Housing benefit and Universal Credit (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [housing, housing-benefit, universal-credit, financial-help]
summary:
- Apply for help with rent through Housing Benefit or Universal Credit.
- Eligibility depends on income, savings, and circumstances.

key_points:
- Most people now claim Universal Credit instead of Housing Benefit.
- Apply online through gov.uk for Universal Credit.
- Working-age people typically use Universal Credit; pensioners may use Housing Benefit.
- Need proof of income, savings, rent amount, and identity.
- Processing time: typically 5 weeks for Universal Credit, 2 weeks for Housing Benefit.
- Payments are usually made directly to you, not the landlord.
- Report any changes in circumstances immediately.
- Discretionary Housing Payments available if you need extra help.
