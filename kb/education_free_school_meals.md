title: Free school meals (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [education, free-school-meals, benefits, children]
summary:
- Children may qualify for free school meals based on family income.
- Apply online through the council website.

key_points:
- Eligible if you receive certain benefits (Universal Credit, Income Support, etc.).
- Universal Credit: income must be less than £7,400 per year.
- All children in reception, year 1, and year 2 get free meals automatically.
- Apply online with proof of benefits and child's details.
- Processing time: typically 5-10 working days.
- Free meals continue as long as you remain eligible.
- Schools receive extra funding for children receiving free meals.
- Report any changes in circumstances to the council.
