title: School admissions (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [education, school-admissions, primary-school, secondary-school]
summary:
- Apply for school places through the council's admissions process.
- Deadlines apply for reception and secondary school applications.

key_points:
- Apply online through the Brighton & Hove Council website.
- Primary school (reception) applications: deadline usually January.
- Secondary school (year 7) applications: deadline usually October.
- List up to 3 preferred schools in order of preference.
- You'll need your child's birth certificate and proof of address.
- Offers are sent on national offer days in April for secondary, April for primary.
- You can appeal if you don't get your preferred school.
- Late applications may reduce your chances of getting preferred schools.
