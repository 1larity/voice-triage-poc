title: Single person Council Tax discount (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [council-tax, discount, single-person]
summary:
- A discount is available when only one adult lives in the property.

key_points:
- Eligible if you’re the only adult (18+) living in the household.
- If you own more than one property, the discount applies to your main home only.
- Discount amount: 25% reduction on the bill.
- While waiting for a decision, you should continue paying; adjustments are made when the discount is applied.
- Applying typically requires your Council Tax account number (found on your bill).
