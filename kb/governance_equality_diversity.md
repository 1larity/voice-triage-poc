title: Equality and diversity policy (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [governance, equality, diversity, inclusion, discrimination]
summary:
- Council committed to equality and eliminating discrimination.
- Policies protect against discrimination based on protected characteristics.

key_points:
- Protected characteristics include: age, disability, gender reassignment, marriage/civil partnership, pregnancy/maternity, race, religion/belief, sex, sexual orientation.
- Council must not discriminate in service delivery or employment.
- Equality Impact Assessment required for new policies and services.
- Public Sector Equality Duty requires council to advance equality.
- Report discrimination or hate incidents to the council.
- Equality and Diversity team oversees implementation.
- Council publishes equality information and objectives.
