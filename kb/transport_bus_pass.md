title: Bus passes and concessionary travel (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [transport, bus-pass, concessionary-travel, older-people]
summary:
- Eligible residents can get free or discounted bus travel.
- Passes available for older people and those with disabilities.

key_points:
- Older people: free bus pass from state pension age.
- Disabled people: may qualify from any age.
- Apply online through the council website or by post.
- Need proof of age, disability, or eligibility.
- Pass allows free travel on off-peak buses in England.
- Some companion passes available for those who need assistance.
- Pass typically arrives within 10 working days.
- Report lost or stolen passes immediately for replacement.
