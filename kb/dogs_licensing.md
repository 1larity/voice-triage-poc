title: Dog ownership and control (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [dogs, pets, dog-control, licensing]
summary:
- Dogs must be kept under control and cleaned up after.
- Certain dogs require a licence or face restrictions.

key_points:
- All dogs must be microchipped and wear a collar with ID tag.
- Clean up after your dog - fines apply for dog fouling.
- Keep dogs under control in public spaces.
- Some areas have dog restrictions or are dog-free.
- Certain breeds are banned under the Dangerous Dogs Act.
- Report dangerous dogs or stray dogs to the council.
- Dogs must not be left alone in hot cars.
- Consider dog training if your dog has behavioural issues.
