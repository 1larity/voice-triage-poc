title: Renting an allotment plot (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [allotments, renting-plot, application]

summary:
- Apply for an allotment plot through Brighton & Hove Council website and pay waiting list fee.
- When plot becomes available, sign tenancy agreement and pay annual rent.

key_points:
- Plots allocated based on waiting list position when tenant leaves.
- Contacted when reach top of waiting list to view available plot.
- Must sign tenancy agreement with site representative to take on plot.
- Welcome pack sent within a month; contact if not received within six weeks.
- Rent year runs from 1 October to 30 September following year.
- Invoices sent in early October; pay by direct debit or online.
- If taking on allotment after 1 October, invoiced from that date to 30 September.
- Must pay within one month of receiving invoice to avoid tenancy termination.

process_steps:
- Find nearest allotment site on city parks map.
- Check plot availability and waiting times for preferred site.
- Apply for allotment and pay waiting list administration fee.
- Wait to be contacted when reach top of waiting list.
- View plot and sign tenancy agreement with site representative.
- Receive welcome pack with tenancy details.
- Set up direct debit or pay annual rent online by invoice due date.

requirements:
- Must pay waiting list administration fee to join waiting list.
- Must sign tenancy agreement when offered a plot.
- Must pay annual rent on time to avoid tenancy termination.
- Must provide correct contact details and keep them up to date.
- Must ensure correct invoice reference number when paying online.

fees:
- Waiting list administration fee required to join waiting list (amount not specified on page).
- Annual allotment rent invoiced in early October for rent year 1 October to 30 September.
- Rent varies by plot size and location.
- Voluntary donations accepted online; can pay more than standard annual rent to support other plot holders.

timelines:
- Average waiting time is 3 to 4 years, sometimes longer for smaller sites.
- Welcome pack sent within a month of signing tenancy agreement.
- Contact allotments team if welcome pack not received within six weeks.
- Rent must be paid within one month of receiving invoice to avoid tenancy termination.
- Can check waiting list position 3 weeks after reserving a plot.

exceptions:
- Some waiting lists are temporarily closed for smaller sites when applicants exceed total number of plots.
- Limited mobility plots (10 sqm with raised beds) only available on Coldean, Foredown and Weald sites.
- Easy access plots not available on all sites.
- Quarter plot holders must reach suitable cultivation level after one year to move to larger plot.
- If taking on allotment after 1 October, invoiced from that date to 30 September.
