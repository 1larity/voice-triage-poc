title: Renting an allotment plot (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [allotments, renting-plot, application]
summary:
- Apply for an allotment plot through the Brighton & Hove Council website.
- Plots are allocated based on availability and waiting lists.

key_points:
- Apply online for an allotment plot in your preferred area.
- Provide your contact details and preferred site(s).
- Most sites have waiting lists; time varies by location.
- You'll be contacted when a plot becomes available.
- Tenancy agreements are typically for 12 months.
- Rent is paid annually in advance.
- New tenants may need to clear overgrown plots.
- You can request a specific plot size if available.
