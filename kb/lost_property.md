title: Lost property (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [lost-property, found-items, police, council]
summary:
- Report lost items to the police or relevant authority.
- Check if your item has been found.

key_points:
- Report lost items to Sussex Police online or by phone.
- Council may hold items found in public buildings or parks.
- Provide detailed description of lost item and where lost.
- Keep checking back as items may be handed in later.
- Some items held for 28 days, others longer.
- Valuable items: report to police immediately.
- Check lost property offices at transport hubs and venues.
- Police and council don't charge for returning found items.
