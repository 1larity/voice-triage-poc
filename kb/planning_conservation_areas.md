summary:
- Special planning rules apply in conservation areas and for listed buildings.
- Additional permissions needed for alterations and demolitions.

key_points:
- Brighton & Hove has many conservation areas with special protection.
- Listed buildings have extra controls to protect historic character.
- Check if your property is in a conservation area or listed.
- Conservation area consent needed for demolition and some alterations.
- Listed building consent needed for any changes to listed buildings.
- Stricter rules on windows, doors, and external alterations.
- Trees in conservation areas have additional protection.
- Penalties for unauthorised work to listed buildings.
