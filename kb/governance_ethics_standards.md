title: Council ethics and standards (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [governance, ethics, standards, conduct]
summary:
- Councillors and staff must follow ethical standards and codes of conduct.
- Independent oversight ensures accountability and transparency.

key_points:
- Councillors follow the Members' Code of Conduct.
- Staff follow the Council's Code of Conduct and Employee Handbook.
- Standards include: honesty, integrity, impartiality, and accountability.
- Register of interests maintained for councillors and senior staff.
- Independent monitoring officer oversees conduct complaints.
- Breaches of standards can result in sanctions or disciplinary action.
- Public can report concerns about councillor conduct.
- Whistleblowing policy protects staff reporting misconduct.
