title: Children and family social care (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [social-care, children, families, child-protection]
summary:
- Support available for children and families in need.
- Contact the council if you're concerned about a child's welfare.

key_points:
- Call the Children's Services team for advice and support.
- Early help available for families facing difficulties.
- Services include: family support, disability support, safeguarding.
- Report concerns about a child's welfare to the MASH (Multi-Agency Safeguarding Hub).
- Emergency concerns: call the out-of-hours duty team or police.
- Support is confidential and focused on keeping families together.
- Social workers assess needs and create support plans.
- You can self-refer or be referred by a professional.
