title: Adult social care assessment (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [social-care, adult-care, assessment, support]
summary:
- Request a care needs assessment if you need help with daily living.
- Assessment determines what support you're eligible for.

key_points:
- Apply online or call the Adult Social Care team.
- Assessment is free and available to anyone over 18.
- Assesses your needs for personal care, household tasks, and staying independent.
- A social worker or occupational therapist may visit you at home.
- Assessment considers your physical, mental, and emotional needs.
- If eligible, you'll receive a care plan outlining support.
- Financial assessment may determine if you need to contribute to costs.
- You can appeal if you disagree with the assessment outcome.
