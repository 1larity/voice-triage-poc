key_points:
- The city is divided into parking zones (A, B, C, etc.).
- Each zone has different permit costs and restrictions.
- Controlled parking zones (CPZ) have specific operating hours.
- Single yellow lines: no parking during controlled hours.
- Double yellow lines: no parking at any time.
- White bay markings: specific parking restrictions apply.
- Check the council website to find your parking zone.
- Restrictions may change during events or roadworks.
