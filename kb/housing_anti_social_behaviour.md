title: Report anti-social behaviour (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [housing, anti-social-behaviour, noise, nuisance, report]
summary:
- Report anti-social behaviour to the council for investigation and action.
- Keep records of incidents to support your case.

key_points:
- Report online, by phone, or in person at your local housing office.
- Anti-social behaviour includes: noise, harassment, vandalism, drug use.
- Provide details: dates, times, what happened, who was involved.
- Keep a diary of incidents with as much detail as possible.
- The council will investigate and may take action against perpetrators.
- In emergencies, call the police on 999.
- Serious cases may lead to eviction or legal action.
- Your identity will be kept confidential where possible.
