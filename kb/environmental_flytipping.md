title: Report fly-tipping (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [environmental, fly-tipping, illegal-dumping, report]
summary:
- Report fly-tipping to the council for investigation and removal.
- Provide as much detail as possible about the incident.

key_points:
- Report fly-tipping online, by phone, or using the council app.
- Include location, description of waste, and any vehicle information.
- Take photos if safe to do so.
- Council will investigate and remove the waste.
- Large or hazardous waste may take longer to remove.
- Don't touch the waste - it may be dangerous.
- Report anonymously if you prefer.
- Fines and prosecution for those caught fly-tipping.
