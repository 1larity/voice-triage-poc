title: Taxi and private hire licence (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [licensing, taxi-licence, private-hire, driver]

summary:
- Apply for a driver, vehicle or operator taxi licence.
- Tell us about a change, report an accident or apply to be on hackney carriage waiting list.

key_points:
- Need a licence issued by the local Licensing Authority (the council) to drive a hackney carriage or private hire vehicle.
- Can hold both hackney carriage and private hire licences at the same time.
- First-time applicants must complete the application form.
- Driver licence is for 3 years.
- Vehicle and operator licences also required.
- Fees apply for all licences.
- Strict rules on vehicle standards and driver conduct.
- Licences can be suspended or revoked for breaches.

process_steps:
- Complete online application form with required documents.
- Attend interview appointment at Hove Town Hall to verify documents and identity.
- Complete taxi or private hire driving assessment (equivalent to DSA/DVSA test).
- Complete Knowledge Tests (Brighton test and Hove test).
- Complete Route test (practical exam).
- Final review of application before licence is granted.

requirements:
- Three forms of identification: passport, birth certificate, or other passport with current visa, utility bill or bank statement.
- DVLA driving licence.
- Passport sized photo (jpeg format).
- Enhanced DBS certificate from TaxiPlus for Brighton & Hove City Council.
- HMRC 9-character tax check code.
- CSE certificate (Blue Lamp Trust Taxi Safeguarding course).
- Wheelchair accessible vehicle drivers need additional certificates.
- Sign up to DBS Update Service and update council every 6 months.
- Pass Versant English Test (first test included in fee).
- Authorisation to work in UK (points-based system check).
- EU Settlement Scheme Certificate if applicable.

fees:
- £150 for first application (administration costs only, not refundable).
- £71 for DBS and DVLA check via TaxiPlus.
- £16 annual payment on receipt of DBS certificate to register with DBS Update Service.
- £49 for Knowledge test.
- £77 for Route test.
- £30 for Child exploitation course (Blue Lamp Trust).
- Language test retakes charged separately.

timelines:
- Minimum of 12 weeks to process application.
- One year from application submission to complete all requirements.
- Complete Hove Knowledge Test and Route Test within 8 weeks of passing Brighton test.
- DBS certificate must be supplied within 28 days of being issued.
- DBS recheck required every 6 months.

exceptions:
- Student visa holders unable to work as self-employed persons will have applications refused.
- Licences can be suspended or revoked for breaches of vehicle standards or driver conduct.
