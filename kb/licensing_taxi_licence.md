title: Taxi and private hire licence (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [licensing, taxi-licence, private-hire, driver]
summary:
- Apply for a taxi or private hire driver licence.
- Vehicle and operator licences also required.

key_points:
- Apply through the council's licensing department.
- Need driving licence, DBS check, medical assessment, and knowledge test.
- Driver licence valid for 3 years.
- Vehicle licence required: MOT, insurance, and vehicle inspection.
- Operator licence required for running a taxi business.
- Fees apply for all licences.
- Strict rules on vehicle standards and driver conduct.
- Licences can be suspended or revoked for breaches.
