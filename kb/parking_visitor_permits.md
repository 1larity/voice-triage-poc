title: Visitor parking permits (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [parking, visitor-permit, guests]
summary:
- Residents can purchase visitor permits for guests.
- Available as digital permits or scratch cards.

key_points:
- Buy visitor permits online through MyAccount.
- Cost is typically £1-£2 per day.
- Digital permits are activated online for specific dates.
- Scratch cards must be displayed in the vehicle.
- Maximum number of visitor permits per household per year.
- Visitors must park in the correct zone.
- Permits are not valid in pay and display bays.
- Check your zone's specific rules on the council website.
