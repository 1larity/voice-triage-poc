title: Planning searches and history (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [planning, planning-search, property-history, searches]
summary:
- Search planning history for a property or area.
- Useful when buying a property or checking previous applications.

key_points:
- Search planning applications online through the council website.
- Search by address, postcode, or planning reference number.
- View application details, drawings, and decisions.
- Historical records available back several decades.
- Conveyancing searches available for property purchases.
- Charges apply for official search certificates.
- Some sensitive information may be redacted.
- Contact planning department if you can't find what you need.
