title: Contact the council (Brighton & Hove)

summary:
- Contact Brighton & Hove Council for help with any service.
- Multiple ways to get in touch depending on your need.

key_points:
- Phone: general enquiries line available during office hours.
- Online: use the council website for most services and reporting.
- In person: visit customer service centres at Hove Town Hall or Bartholomew House.
- Email: available for some services - check website for specific addresses.
- Social media: council active on Twitter and Facebook for updates.
- Emergency out-of-hours: specific number for urgent issues.
- Have your postcode and account details ready when contacting.
- Wait times may vary - online services often quickest.
