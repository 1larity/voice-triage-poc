title: Register a birth (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [registrars, birth-registration, baby, certificates]
summary:
- Register your baby's birth within 42 days at the register office.
- You'll need to book an appointment and bring specific documents.

key_points:
- Book an appointment online or by phone at the register office.
- Must register within 42 days of the birth.
- Bring: birth notification from hospital, ID for parents, proof of address.
- Both parents should attend if married; if unmarried, both must attend for father's details.
- Registration takes about 30 minutes.
- Birth certificate costs around £11 per copy.
- You can buy multiple certificates at the time of registration.
- Register in the area where the baby was born.
