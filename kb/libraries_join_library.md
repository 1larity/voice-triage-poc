title: Join the library (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [libraries, library-card, membership, books]
summary:
- Join Brighton & Hove libraries for free access to books, digital resources, and more.
- Join online or in person at any library.

key_points:
- Join online through the library website or visit any library.
- Bring proof of ID and address if joining in person.
- Membership is free for all residents.
- Borrow up to 20 items at a time.
- Loan periods: 3 weeks for most items, 1 week for DVDs.
- Renew items online, by phone, or in person.
- Access e-books, audiobooks, and magazines online.
- Use your card at all Brighton & Hove libraries.
