key_points:
- Parks generally open from dawn to dusk.
- Dogs must be kept under control and cleaned up after.
- Some areas have dog-free zones or time restrictions.
- No cycling on paths unless designated cycle routes.
- BBQs and fires generally prohibited in parks.
- No fishing without permit in park lakes and ponds.
- Respect wildlife and don't damage trees or plants.
- Report vandalism or anti-social behaviour to the council.
