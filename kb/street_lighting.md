title: Report street lighting problems (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [street-lighting, lights, report, maintenance]
summary:
- Report faulty street lights to the council for repair.
- Include location and description of the problem.

key_points:
- Report street light problems online or by phone.
- Provide: exact location, what's wrong (light out, flickering, damaged).
- Include the lamp post number if visible.
- Emergency repairs (damaged or dangerous lights) prioritized.
- Routine repairs typically completed within 10 working days.
- Report lights that stay on during daylight hours.
- Council maintains all public street lighting.
- Private lighting is property owner's responsibility.
