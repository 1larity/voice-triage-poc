title: Parking fines and appeals (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [parking, parking-fine, PCN, appeal]
summary:
- Parking fines (PCNs) can be paid online or challenged if you believe they were issued incorrectly.
- Act quickly - discounts apply for early payment.

key_points:
- Pay or challenge within 28 days of receiving the PCN.
- Pay within 14 days for a 50% discount.
- Challenge online, by post, or by email.
- Grounds for appeal include: incorrect ticket, vehicle stolen, broken meter, permit displayed.
- Provide evidence to support your appeal.
- If appeal is rejected, you can appeal to the independent adjudicator.
- Unpaid fines may increase and lead to court action.
- Check the PCN for specific payment and appeal instructions.
