title: Entertainment and late night licences (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [licensing, entertainment-licence, late-night, events]
summary:
- Entertainment licences needed for live music, films, and late-night refreshment.
- Apply through the council's licensing department.

key_points:
- Premises licence covers: live music, recorded music, films, dancing, late-night hot food.
- Temporary event notice for one-off events (up to 500 people, 168 hours).
- Apply at least 10 working days before temporary event.
- Permanent licence application: 2-3 months processing time.
- Public notice required for 28 days for permanent licences.
- Police, fire service, and environmental health consulted.
- Strict conditions on noise control and security.
- Licences can be reviewed or revoked for breaches.
