title: Allotment rules and regulations (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [allotments, rules, regulations, tenancy]
summary:
- Allotment tenants must follow council rules to maintain their plot.
- Rules cover cultivation, structures, and site conduct.

key_points:
- Plots must be cultivated and kept free of weeds.
- No permanent structures without permission.
- Sheds and greenhouses allowed with prior approval.
- No livestock or animals on allotments.
- Bonfires are restricted; check site-specific rules.
- Water must be used responsibly.
- Keep paths and boundaries tidy.
- Respect neighbours and follow site etiquette.
- Tenancy can be terminated for rule breaches.
- Subletting is not permitted.
