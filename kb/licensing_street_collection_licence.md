title: Street collection and charity licences (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [licensing, street-collection, charity, fundraising]
summary:
- Need a licence to collect money or sell goods on the street.
- Separate licences for charities and commercial activities.

key_points:
- Apply to the council's licensing department for street collection licence.
- Charity collections: need to be registered charity or have charitable purpose.
- Commercial street trading: separate licence required.
- Apply at least 28 days before planned collection.
- Specify dates, times, and locations for collection.
- Collectors must carry ID and licence while collecting.
- Strict rules on where and when collections can take place.
- Return collected money within specified timeframe.
