title: Emergency preparedness and severe weather (Brighton & Hove)

summary:
- Council provides information and support during emergencies and severe weather.
- Be prepared and know who to contact.

key_points:
- Check council website for emergency information and updates.
- Severe weather: gritting, school closures, travel advice.
- Flooding: report issues and get advice from Environment Agency.
- Council works with emergency services during major incidents.
- Sign up for alerts for weather warnings and emergencies.
- Emergency accommodation available if homes become uninhabitable.
- Report dangerous situations to 999.
- Keep emergency kit with essentials.
