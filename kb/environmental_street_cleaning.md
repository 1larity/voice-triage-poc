title: Street cleaning and litter (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [environmental, street-cleaning, litter, report]
summary:
- Report litter or street cleaning issues to the council.
- Streets are cleaned on a scheduled basis.

key_points:
- Report litter, dog mess, or street cleaning issues online or by phone.
- Provide exact location and description of the problem.
- Streets are cleaned according to a schedule based on usage.
- High streets and town centres cleaned daily.
- Residential streets cleaned weekly or fortnightly.
- Missed cleans can be reported for investigation.
- Council provides litter bins throughout the city.
- Report overflowing bins or damaged bins for attention.
