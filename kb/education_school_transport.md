title: School transport and travel assistance (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [education, school-transport, travel-assistance, buses]
summary:
- Help with school transport may be available for eligible children.
- Criteria include distance, age, and special educational needs.

key_points:
- Free transport if walking distance is over 2 miles (primary) or 3 miles (secondary).
- Children with special educational needs may qualify regardless of distance.
- Low-income families may get help with transport costs.
- Apply online through the council website.
- Provide proof of address, school details, and any relevant medical information.
- Transport is usually by dedicated school bus or public transport pass.
- Appeals can be made if transport is refused.
- Review your eligibility annually as circumstances may change.
