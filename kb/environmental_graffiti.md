title: Report graffiti (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [environmental, graffiti, vandalism, report]
summary:
- Report graffiti to the council for removal.
- Offensive or racist graffiti is prioritized.

key_points:
- Report graffiti online or by phone.
- Provide location and description of the graffiti.
- Include photos if possible.
- Offensive, racist, or homophobic graffiti removed within 24 hours.
- Other graffiti typically removed within 10 working days.
- Graffiti on private property: owner may need to arrange removal.
- Council may charge for repeated graffiti on the same property.
- Report anonymously if you prefer.
