title: Report potholes and road problems (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [roads, potholes, highways, report]
summary:
- Report potholes and road defects to the council for repair.
- Include location and severity of the problem.

key_points:
- Report potholes online or by phone.
- Provide: exact location, size, and depth of pothole.
- Include photos if possible.
- Dangerous potholes prioritized for repair.
- Minor defects may be monitored or scheduled for routine repair.
- Report other road issues: damaged manholes, broken kerbs, faded road markings.
- Council maintains all public roads and pavements.
- Private roads are property owners' responsibility.
