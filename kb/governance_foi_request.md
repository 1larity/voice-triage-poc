title: Freedom of Information requests (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [governance, FOI, freedom-of-information, transparency]
summary:
- Request information from the council under the Freedom of Information Act.
- Most requests responded to within 20 working days.

key_points:
- Submit FOI requests online through the council website or by email.
- Specify exactly what information you need.
- Council must respond within 20 working days.
- Some information may be exempt from disclosure.
- You can appeal if your request is refused or you're unhappy with the response.
- Personal data requests should use GDPR process, not FOI.
- Some requests may incur fees for large amounts of information.
- Check if information is already published before making a request.
