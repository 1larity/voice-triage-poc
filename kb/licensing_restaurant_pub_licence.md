title: Restaurant and pub licences (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [licensing, restaurant-licence, pub-licence, food-business]
summary:
- Food businesses need to register with the council and may need a premises licence.
- Separate requirements for food hygiene and alcohol sales.

key_points:
- Register food business with environmental health within 28 days of opening.
- Food registration is free but mandatory.
- Premises licence needed if selling alcohol or providing late-night refreshment.
- Apply through the council's licensing department.
- Need floor plans, operating schedule, and details of proposed activities.
- Food hygiene rating given after inspection.
- Alcohol licence requires personal licence holder on site.
- Licences can be reviewed if problems occur.
