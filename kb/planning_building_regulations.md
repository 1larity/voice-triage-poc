title: Building regulations approval (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [planning, building-regulations, construction, approval]
summary:
- Building regulations ensure construction work meets safety standards.
- Separate from planning permission but often required together.

key_points:
- Apply through the council's Building Control service.
- Required for most structural changes, extensions, and new builds.
- Covers: structural safety, fire safety, insulation, drainage, accessibility.
- Submit detailed plans and specifications.
- Building control inspectors visit site at key stages.
- Approval certificate issued when work meets regulations.
- Fees apply based on project value and type.
- Work without approval may need to be rectified or removed.
