title: Get married or register a civil partnership (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [registrars, marriage, civil-partnership, wedding]
summary:
- Give notice of marriage or civil partnership at the register office.
- Book your ceremony at least 28 days in advance.

key_points:
- Book an appointment to give notice at the register office.
- Must give notice at least 28 days before the ceremony.
- Bring: ID, proof of address, proof of nationality, and if divorced, decree absolute.
- Notice valid for 12 months.
- Ceremony can be at the register office or approved venue.
- Fees apply for notice and ceremony.
- Both partners must give notice separately.
- Ceremonies available Monday to Saturday, some Sundays.
