title: Alcohol premises licence (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [licensing, alcohol-licence, premises-licence, business]
summary:
- Apply for a premises licence to sell alcohol.
- Required for pubs, restaurants, shops, and events.

key_points:
- Apply through the council's licensing department.
- Need detailed operating schedule, floor plans, and layout.
- Application fee varies by rateable value.
- Public notice required for 28 days.
- Police, fire service, and other authorities consulted.
- Processing time: typically 2-3 months.
- Licence can be reviewed or revoked if problems occur.
- Personal licence also required for individuals selling alcohol.
