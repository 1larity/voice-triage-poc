title: Council organised events (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [events, council-events, festivals, community]
summary:
- Council organises and supports various events throughout the year.
- Check the council website for upcoming events and festivals.

key_points:
- Major events include: Brighton Festival, Pride, Burning the Clocks.
- Council provides permits and support for community events.
- Event organisers may need licences for alcohol, entertainment, and road closures.
- Apply for event permits well in advance - typically 3-6 months.
- Council may charge for event services like waste collection and security.
- Check what events are happening on the council website.
- Some events are free, others require tickets.
- Volunteer opportunities available at many council events.
