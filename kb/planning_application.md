title: Planning permission application (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [planning, planning-permission, building, application]
summary:
- Apply for planning permission through the council's planning portal.
- Most building work requires planning permission.

key_points:
- Apply online through the Planning Portal or council website.
- Need detailed plans, drawings, and location maps.
- Application fee varies by project type and size.
- Processing time: typically 8 weeks for householder applications, 13 weeks for major applications.
- Neighbours will be consulted and can comment.
- Planning committee decides on major or controversial applications.
- You can appeal if permission is refused.
- Check if you need permission before starting work.
