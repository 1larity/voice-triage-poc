title: Council complaints procedure (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [governance, complaints, feedback, service-issues]
summary:
- Complain about council services if you're unhappy with the service received.
- Council aims to resolve complaints within set timescales.

key_points:
- Make complaints online, by phone, email, or in writing.
- Include details: what happened, when, who you dealt with, and what you want to happen.
- Acknowledgement sent within 3 working days.
- Response within 10 working days for most complaints.
- Complex complaints may take up to 20 working days.
- If unhappy with response, you can request a review.
- Local Government Ombudsman can review unresolved complaints.
- Complaining won't affect your access to services.
