- Parks include: Preston Park, Queen's Park, Hove Park, and many more.
- Most parks open dawn to dusk year-round.
- Book sports pitches, tennis courts, and event spaces online.
- Events may require permits and insurance.
- Report vandalism, litter, or maintenance issues to the council.
- Dogs allowed in most parks but must be kept under control.
- Some areas have dog restrictions or designated dog-free zones.
- Check park rules before booking events.
