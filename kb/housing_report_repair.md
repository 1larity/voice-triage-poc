title: Report a council housing repair (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [housing, repairs, council-housing, maintenance]
summary:
- Report repairs to council housing online, by phone, or in person.
- Emergency repairs are prioritized and dealt with quickly.

key_points:
- Report repairs online through MyAccount or call the repairs hotline.
- Emergency repairs: completed within 24 hours (e.g., no heating, burst pipes).
- Urgent repairs: completed within 7 days (e.g., broken windows, electrical issues).
- Routine repairs: completed within 28 days (e.g., minor leaks, plastering).
- Provide your address, contact details, and description of the problem.
- You may need to be home for the repair appointment.
- Tenants are responsible for minor repairs and damage they caused.
- Keep records of repair requests for reference.
