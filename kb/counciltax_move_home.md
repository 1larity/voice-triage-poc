title: Tell us you’re moving (Council Tax – Brighton & Hove)
tags: [council-tax, change-of-address, moving-home]
summary:
- Updating your address is handled through MyAccount.

key_points:
- Sign in (or register) for MyAccount.
- Go to personal details → update your address → edit → enter new address → submit.
- Account details update immediately unless your move-in date is in the future.
- The council states this is the quickest way to update your Council Tax account and a new bill will be issued.

demo_questions:
- “I’m moving — how do I update my Council Tax address?”
- “My move date is next month — will it update immediately?”
