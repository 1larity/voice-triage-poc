title: Register to vote (UK – practical basics)
tags: [voting, register-to-vote, change-of-address]
summary:
- Registering can be done online and should be redone after moving.

key_points:
- Online registration is designed to take only a few minutes and typically requires a National Insurance number.
- You must register at the address where you live.
- If you move house, you should register to vote again.
- If you can’t register online, there is a paper form option.
