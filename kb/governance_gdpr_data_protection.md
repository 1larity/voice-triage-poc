title: GDPR and data protection (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [governance, GDPR, data-protection, privacy]
summary:
- Council protects personal data under GDPR and Data Protection Act.
- You have rights to access, correct, and delete your personal data.

key_points:
- Council must handle personal data lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
- You can request a copy of your personal data (subject access request).
- Respond to subject access requests within one month.
- You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
- You can request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.
- Council must report data breaches to the Information Commissioner's Office.
- Data protection officer oversees council's compliance.
- Contact the council's data protection team for data concerns.
