title: Clinical and medical waste collection (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [bins, clinical-waste, medical-waste]

summary:
- Council provides clinical waste collection for households producing medical waste.
- Service available for offensive waste, infectious waste, and sharps.

key_points:
- Medical professionals caring for you at home should take clinical waste away themselves.
- If NHS professional leaves waste with you, phone NHS on 01273 242 077 (option 2) to arrange collection.
- Council collects for residents producing clinical waste at home without medical professional care.
- Three collection types: offensive (tiger bags), infectious (orange bags), sharps (yellow boxes).
- Phone council on 01273 292 929 if information applies to you.
- Minimum 2 tiger bags required for collection.
- Less than 2 bags can be double bagged and placed in normal bin (no needles/syringes).
- No clinical waste from pets collected.

process_steps:
- Phone council on 01273 292 929 to request clinical waste collection.
- Council passes details to Medisort (contractor).
- Medisort phones back within 48 hours to book collection day.
- Prepare waste: tie bags, seal sharps boxes, do not place in normal bin.
- Present waste for collection Monday to Wednesday, 7am to 2pm.

requirements:
- Must produce clinical waste at home without care of medical professional.
- Must meet minimum quantities: 2+ tiger bags for offensive waste.
- Must tie bags and seal sharps boxes.
- Must not place infectious or non-infectious waste in normal bin.
- Must not include pet clinical waste.

fees:
- UNKNOWN for clinical waste collection fees.

timelines:
- Medisort phones back within 48 hours to book collection.
- Collections take place Monday to Wednesday, 7am to 2pm.
- UNKNOWN for collection frequency or ongoing schedule.

exceptions:
- Medical professionals should take clinical waste away themselves.
- If NHS professional leaves waste, phone NHS on 01273 242 077 (option 2).
- Council won't collect less than 2 tiger bags.
- Council won't collect clinical waste relating to pets.
- Offensive waste only collected if producing more than 2 bags per week.
- Infectious waste only collected if hospital or GP referred you.

contacts:
- Council: 01273 292 929 for clinical waste collection requests.
- Medisort (contractor): 01903 719 646 or ask@medisort.co.uk after booking confirmed.
- NHS: 01273 242 077 (option 2) if NHS professional left waste with you.
- Response time: Medisort phones back within 48 hours.
