title: Clinical and medical waste collection (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [bins, clinical-waste, medical-waste]
summary:
- Clinical waste collection is available for residents with medical needs.
- Requires a referral from a healthcare professional.

key_points:
- Clinical waste includes sharps, dressings, and other medical waste.
- Request collection through your GP, nurse, or healthcare professional.
- Yellow bags and sharps boxes are provided for clinical waste.
- Collections are usually weekly.
- Service is free for eligible residents.
- Do not put clinical waste in regular bins.
- Contact the council directly if you need to arrange clinical waste collection.
