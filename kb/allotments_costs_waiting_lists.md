title: Allotment costs and waiting lists (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [allotments, costs, waiting-list, fees]
summary:
- Allotment rents vary by plot size and location.
- Waiting lists exist for most sites; check current wait times online.

key_points:
- Standard plot (approximately 250 square metres): around £50-£80 per year.
- Half plots are available at reduced rates.
- Concessions available for pensioners and those on benefits.
- Water charges may apply separately.
- Waiting list times vary from months to years depending on site.
- Check the council website for current waiting list lengths.
- You can join multiple site waiting lists.
- Rent is reviewed annually and may increase.
