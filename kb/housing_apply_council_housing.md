title: Apply for council housing (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [housing, council-housing, application, social-housing]
summary:
- Apply for council housing through the HomeChoice Brighton & Hove bidding system.
- Need to meet eligibility criteria and provide supporting documents.

key_points:
- Register online at HomeChoice Brighton & Hove website.
- Must be 18 or over and have a local connection to Brighton & Hove.
- Provide proof of identity, income, and current housing situation.
- Applications are assessed and placed in priority bands.
- Bid on available properties that match your needs.
- Processing time: typically 2-4 weeks for initial assessment.
- Keep your details updated to maintain your position.
- Priority given to those in urgent housing need.
