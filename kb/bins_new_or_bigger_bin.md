title: Request a new/replacement bin or ask for a different size (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [bins, replacement, larger-bin]
summary:
- Residents can request bins/boxes online and may request a different size.

key_points:
- You can request a new wheelie bin or black box online; most properties get a standard size by default.
- You can request a replacement if your bin/box is damaged or lost.
- If asking for a bigger general-waste bin, the council may need more information and may inspect waste/recycling habits.
- Put your property number on bins to reduce losses.

demo_questions:
- “My bin is missing/damaged — how do I get another?”
- “Can I get a bigger bin?”
