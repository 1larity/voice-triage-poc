title: What goes in each bin (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [bins, recycling, waste-sorting]
summary:
- Different bins are for different types of waste.
- Check the council website for detailed lists of what can be recycled.

key_points:
- Grey/lidded bin: General non-recyclable waste.
- Green bin: Paper and card recycling (not plastic bottles or cans).
- Blue bin: Mixed recycling including plastic bottles, cans, glass, and cartons.
- Brown bin: Garden waste (subscription service).
- Food waste caddy: Food waste including cooked and uncooked food.
- Black box: Paper and card for some properties.
- Rinse containers before recycling.
- Flatten cardboard boxes to save space.
- No plastic bags in recycling bins - use clear bags only if specified.
