title: Register a death (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [registrars, death-registration, bereavement, certificates]
summary:
- Register a death within 5 days at the register office.
- You'll need the medical certificate and other documents.

key_points:
- Book an appointment online or by phone at the register office.
- Must register within 5 days (8 days in Scotland).
- Bring: medical certificate of cause of death, birth certificate, marriage certificate, NHS medical card.
- Person registering should be a relative or present at the death.
- Registration takes about 30 minutes.
- Death certificate costs around £11 per copy.
- You'll get a certificate for burial or cremation.
- Tell the registrar if you want the Tell Us Once service.
