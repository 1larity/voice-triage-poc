title: Order birth, death and marriage certificates (Brighton & Hove)
tags: [registrars, certificates, birth-certificate, death-certificate, marriage-certificate]
summary:
- Order copies of birth, death, and marriage certificates from the register office.
- Certificates needed for official purposes like passports and benefits.

key_points:
- Order online, by phone, or in person at the register office.
- Provide full details: name, date, and place of event.
- Standard certificates cost around £11 each.
- Priority service available for faster delivery (additional cost).
- Processing time: standard 5-10 working days, priority 1-2 days.
- Need to know which register office holds the record.
- Certificates can be posted or collected in person.
- Bring ID if collecting in person.
